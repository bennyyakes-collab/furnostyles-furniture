How To Maintain Your Furniture So It Lasts Longer

Furniture advice from Furnostyles Furniture for homes, offices and commercial spaces in Kenya.

Proper furniture care keeps your sofas, beds, wardrobes, tables and cabinets looking better for longer.

Why This Matters

Many damages start small: loose screws, weak hinges, stains, moisture, direct sun and poor movement handling.

Regular cleaning, careful usage and quick repair help extend furniture life.

What To Consider

Keep furniture away from excessive moisture, avoid dragging heavy pieces and clean fabric based on its material type.

Repair small damages early before they become costly.
