About Furnostyles Furniture

Furnostyles Furniture is a professional custom furniture brand in Nairobi, Kenya, focused on modern furniture design, durable construction, clean finishing and comfortable living spaces. We create furniture that improves how a home or business looks, feels and functions.

Our work includes custom sofas, beds, TV stands, wardrobes, dining sets, office desks, shelving, storage units and furniture repair services. We serve clients who want furniture that is beautiful, practical and made to fit their exact space.

We believe furniture should be strong, neat, comfortable and visually balanced. Every piece is planned with measurements, material choice, color harmony, usage needs and finishing quality in mind.

Our Craftsmanship

Our process starts with understanding your space and the purpose of the furniture. From there, we guide you on suitable designs, sizes, fabrics, colors and finishing options. Whether you are furnishing a new home, upgrading your sitting room, repairing an old sofa or planning office furniture, we aim to deliver a premium result.

Furnostyles Furniture is built around comfort, quality and elegance. That is the standard we bring into every project.
