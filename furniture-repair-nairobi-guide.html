Furniture Repair In Nairobi: When To Repair Instead Of Buying New

Furniture advice from Furnostyles Furniture for homes, offices and commercial spaces in Kenya.

Furniture repair is a smart option when the frame is still useful but fabric, cushions, hinges, drawers, polish or support parts are damaged.

Why This Matters

Many sofas, beds, chairs, wardrobes and cabinets can be restored at a lower cost than buying new furniture.

Our hotcake repair service helps clients refresh existing furniture through upholstery repair, foam replacement, frame reinforcement and surface finishing.

What To Consider

Repair is best when the furniture has good structure, sentimental value, special size, or when only specific parts are damaged.

We check the damage, explain repair options and help you decide whether repair or replacement gives better value.
